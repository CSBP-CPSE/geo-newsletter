November Newsletter

Welcome to the first edition of GeoNewsletter! Designed for Field 5 by the Data Exploration and Integration Lab, approximately each month the latest geomatics publications, technical resources, developments and applications will be delivered. This month’s newsletter contains resources that were curated in effort to bridge the world of geomatics to ongoing projects at Statistics Canada.

As we strive to cater this newsletter for Field 5, please feel free to reach out to Julia Conzon or Marina Smailes with specific topics/themes to include in future releases.

Agriculture

H2O Geomatics: Using Remote Sensing Technology to Improve Farmer’s Yields with NXT Farm

Using Earth-Orbit data, NXT Farm web application notifies users of crop health conditions and offers variable rate fertilization plans. NXT Farm includes both NDVI and custom algorithms that parametrize crop nitrogen levels, water stress, and soil moisture.

Mid-season Crop Classification Using Dual-, Compact-, and Full-Polarization in Preparation for the Radarsat Constellation Mission (RCM)

Comparing crop classification accuracy of varying polarized Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) data of Manitoba using pixel- and object-based random forest algorithms.
